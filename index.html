Our Menu

Dish 1

A beloved Indian dish, Chicken Tikka Masala consists of tender chunks of marinated chicken, grilled to perfection and simmered in a creamy tomato-based sauce. The rich gravy is flavored with a blend of traditional spices, including cumin, coriander, and garam masala, creating a dish that's both hearty and flavorful. Served with naan or basmati rice, it's a true culinary delight.

Dish 2

A crispy, golden-brown South Indian crepe made from a fermented batter of rice and lentils. The dosa is filled with a spiced potato filling, seasoned with mustard seeds, turmeric, and curry leaves. Served with a side of coconut chutney and tangy sambar, this dish offers a delightful balance of flavors and textures

Dish 3

A popular Indo-Chinese dish, Vegetable Hakka Noodles features stir-fried noodles tossed with a colorful array of fresh vegetables like bell peppers, carrots, cabbage, and spring onions. Infused with a blend of soy sauce, garlic, and ginger, this dish delivers a savory and aromatic experience with every bite.
